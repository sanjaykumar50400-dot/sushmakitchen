Section 10 — Home Tiffin Service

Operated from Sushma's home kitchen — zero additional rent, zero platform commission, zero infrastructure cost. Pure margin income.

Why Tiffin is the Best Revenue Stream

Unlike dine-in (requires customers to come) or Zomato (takes 25% commission), tiffin subscriptions are direct, pre-paid, monthly income with zero platform dependency.

One subscriber from an apartment building typically refers 2–3 neighbours once they're happy. Society WhatsApp groups are the most effective marketing tool — and it's completely free.

Tiffin pricing strategy: ₹2,500/month for lunch + dinner daily (30 days). Customers perceive it as ₹83/meal — far cheaper than ordering from Zomato. You get guaranteed income.

Section 11 — Risk Assessment

What Could Go Wrong
& How We Handle It

Every business has risks. Here we identify them honestly and provide specific, actionable mitigation for each.

📉
Slow Start on Zomato
LOW RISK
New listings take time to build reviews. First 30 days may see only 5–10 orders/day.
✅ Solution: Run Zomato's "New Restaurant" promotions Week 1–4. Ask first 50 customers to review. Offer first 100 delivery customers a free extra item to drive reviews quickly.
☀️
Terrace Unusable in Peak Summer
MEDIUM RISK
April–June in Noida hits 44–46°C. Terrace becomes unusable without shade or cooling.
✅ Solution: Budget ₹5,000–8,000 for a cooler on terrace. Focus delivery orders during peak summer. Summer beverages (shikanjvi, cold coffee, mango shake) become primary revenue. Terrace works beautifully Oct–March.
👩‍🍳
Single Cook Dependency
LOW RISK
If the primary cook is absent, kitchen operations halt.
✅ Solution: Train a second helper from Day 1 on core recipes. Document all recipes with standard quantities. Keep a backup part-time cook contact for emergencies. Sushma herself can cover basic morning menu if needed.
🏁
Competitor Enters the Area
LOW RISK (6–12 months)
Once the market is proven, another kitchen may list on Zomato in Sector 93.
✅ Solution: Build 200+ reviews and 4.3+ rating within 6 months — a near-impossible moat to overcome quickly. Establish society WhatsApp groups and direct tiffin relationships that don't depend on apps.
⭐
Inconsistent Food Quality
LOW RISK
One bad review on Zomato can hurt visibility and reduce orders significantly.
✅ Solution: Keep menu small (20–25 items max) in Phase A. Standardize every recipe with measured quantities. Respond to every review within 24 hours. Never let the rating fall below 4.0.
💸
Cash Flow in Month 1–2
LOW RISK
Revenue in Month 1 may be lower than operating costs while reviews are being built.
✅ Solution: The ₹60,000–1,00,000 working capital buffer in the investment covers 2 months of operational costs. Tiffin subscriptions (pre-paid) provide immediate cash from Day 1 before delivery orders grow.
